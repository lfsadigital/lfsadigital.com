Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

LFSA Digital ("we," "us," "our") operates LFSA Content Manager, a content management tool for scheduling and publishing social media content. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to LFSA Content Manager, we collect and store:

  • OAuth tokens: Access and refresh tokens provided by TikTok to authorize content publishing on your behalf.
  • Basic account information: Your TikTok username, display name, and profile picture, used to identify connected accounts within the dashboard.
  • Account statistics: Follower count and engagement metrics for performance tracking.
  • Video metadata: Information about published videos (view counts, likes, comments) for analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your TikTok account for content publishing.
  • Scheduling and uploading content (photos, videos) to TikTok as drafts on your behalf.
  • Displaying your account information and performance metrics within the dashboard.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your data is transmitted only between LFSA Content Manager and TikTok's API as required to provide the service.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on our self-hosted, secured servers. OAuth tokens are stored encrypted. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is connected to the service. When you disconnect your TikTok account or request deletion, we remove all associated data, including OAuth tokens and cached account information, within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Disconnect your TikTok account from LFSA Content Manager.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your stored data.
  • Revoke LFSA Content Manager's access via your TikTok account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

LFSA Content Manager is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at luiz@lfsadigital.com.
